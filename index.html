Introdution

Raechel Portelli is a geographic information scientist with 17 years of experience in the analysis of geographic data including remotely sensed imagery, GIS, text analysis, and geovisualization. Her career has spanned the University of Idaho, Pennsylvania State University, and Michigan State University. In addition to research, Dr. Portelli has developed and instructed courses in Geographic Object-Based Image Analysis, Cartography, and a variety of specialty directed topics covering GIS analytics.
