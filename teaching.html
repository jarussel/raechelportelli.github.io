Introduction to the course

Welcome to Map Design (GEO 326). This course is an introductory cartography course meant for undergraduate students. It is a 4-credit course and consists of 2 50 minute lectures and 2 50 minute labs per week. You may access the SYLLABUS. The official course description from the registrar's office is "Map design, layout, and usability. Typography and color theory. Techniques of map production, print and digital display."
